First off, I don’t think there’s necessarily a tradeoff between technology and jobs. Other countries avail themselves of the same technology that is at our disposal, yet they have lower unemployment rates (e.g., the Nordic countries). Moreover, technological innovations have been the norm since the first humans “created” fire. Nevertheless, there have been periods of full and below-full employment. I would add that the Bureau of Labor Statistics projects a majority of jobs will be in the service sector, about 80 percent.

As for “social credit,” I skimmed the lengthy Wikipedia entry. There does not seem to be wide scale acceptance of the proposal, and I have no idea if it’s feasible. Above my pay grade, I think.
