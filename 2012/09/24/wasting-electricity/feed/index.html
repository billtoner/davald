]]> By: azleader https://davald.wordpress.com/2012/09/24/wasting-electricity/#comment-1199 Wed, 26 Sep 2012 13:59:01 +0000 http://snohomishobserver.com/?p=4374#comment-1199 Server farms are extreme energy hogs because of their use of spinning platters that hold data. They have to spin at a high rate of speed to access data quickly and you have to have lots of them to handle wild fluctuations in server demand.

The solution is solid-state storage devices. They are a technology whose time has come and are now beginning show up in larger numbers. Their capacity is small now, but will increase.They have no spinning platters and require a fraction of the energy to run.

Pure economics will drive replacing current data storage and access with solid-state devices. When the price point of solid-state drops below energy consuming disk drives you’ll see spinning disks disappear faster than vinyl records.
