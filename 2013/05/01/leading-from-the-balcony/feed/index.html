Comments on: Leading from the balcony https://davald.wordpress.com/2013/05/01/leading-from-the-balcony/ Prima facie observations about anything Mon, 13 May 2013 17:56:39 +0000 hourly 1 http://wordpress.com/ By: Greg https://davald.wordpress.com/2013/05/01/leading-from-the-balcony/#comment-3209 Mon, 13 May 2013 17:56:39 +0000 http://snohomishobserver.com/?p=5724#comment-3209 The “leading from the balcony” crowd has, in some corners, taken this idea to an unhealthy extreme. Gaining all of the perspective you can while leading is a good thing, and necessary in the continuing development of leaders. There is a time, however, when leaders must roll-up their sleeves and, if necessary, get a little dirt on their white collars. People want to see their leaders in the trenches on occasion and you can’t do that if you are in the balcony all day. It takes an astute leader to know when each method is called for and how to balance these tactics.
