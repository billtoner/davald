On the one hand, I am so ANGRY when I see Obama cow-towing to Republican temper-tantrums rather than fighting fire with fire, especially when the tax breaks came up to expire, but on the other hand, I realize that his job is not easy, and kicking and screaming like the right isn’t going to win any battles anyway — just make us all look all the more incompetent.

The problem is that the Republicans have the upper hand in these negotiations (and all those in the past two years) and they know it. They are willing to cut the throats of the American people because they know that no matter the cause, the blame will ultimately fall on Obama and “the Democrats” because that’s the party in the hot seat. This puts Obama is a lose-lose situation. He will either meet Democratic expectations and in the process contribute to the larger problem, or he will bend over the backwards for “compromise” to save what (very little) he can and still come out looking the loser.
